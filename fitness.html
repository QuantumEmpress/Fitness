Boxing

Boxing is a combat sport where two fighters face off in a square ring, aiming to land punches on each other's upper body and head while avoiding their opponent's blows. It combines strategy, skill, and physical prowess, with the goal of either knocking out the opponent or scoring more points through clean punches.
